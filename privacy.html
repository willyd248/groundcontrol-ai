Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 6, 2026 · GroundControl AI
Summary GroundControl AI does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The simulation runs entirely in your browser. No account, login, or personal data is required or gathered.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how GroundControl AI (groundcontrol-ai.vercel.app) handles information when you visit the site. We take a minimal-data approach: the simulation itself collects nothing about you.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

The simulation collects no personally identifiable information (PII). There is no account system, no login, no form submission, and no tracking of individual users within the simulation itself. All simulation state is generated locally in your browser and is never transmitted to any server.

3. Analytics

The site may use Vercel Analytics to collect anonymized, aggregate page view data. This includes information such as page URL, referrer, country-level location, and browser type — but not your IP address or any personally identifying information. Vercel Analytics is cookieless and GDPR-compliant by design.

4. Cookies

GroundControl AI does not set any first-party cookies. One exception: the site stores a single key (gc_seen) in your browser's localStorage to remember whether you have dismissed the onboarding overlay. This value never leaves your device.

Third-party services (Google Fonts, Vercel) may set their own cookies per their respective privacy policies.

5. Third-Party Services

Vercel — hosts this site. Subject to Vercel's Privacy Policy.

Google Fonts — serves the JetBrains Mono typeface. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy. Your IP may be sent to Google to load the font.

6. Future Email Capture

If an email signup feature is added in the future, this policy will be updated prior to launch. Any email collection will be opt-in only, and Resend will be used as the email processor. Subscribers will be able to unsubscribe at any time.

7. Children

This site is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

8. Changes to This Policy

Will DiMaio reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be directed to Will DiMaio via GitHub.
