Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2025 · Effective immediately

Short version: GroundControl AI is a client-side simulation. We do not collect, store, or sell personal data. The only data stored is a single localStorage flag that records whether you have dismissed the onboarding overlay.

This Privacy Policy describes how Will DiMaio ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with GroundControl AI ("the Service") at groundcontrol-ai.vercel.app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to operate the Service:

  • No account or registration data. The Service requires no sign-up, login, or account creation.
  • No personal data. We do not ask for or collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.
  • LocalStorage. The Service stores one item in your browser's localStorage: gc_seen, a boolean flag that records whether you have dismissed the onboarding overlay. This data never leaves your device.

2. Automatically Collected Information

Like most web services, our hosting provider (Vercel) may automatically collect standard server log data when you visit, including:

  • IP address (anonymized or truncated per Vercel's data practices)
  • Browser type and version
  • Referring URL
  • Date and time of access
  • Pages requested

This information is used solely for security monitoring, abuse prevention, and aggregate traffic analytics. It is governed by Vercel's Privacy Policy.

3. Analytics

We may use Vercel Analytics and/or Plausible Analytics to understand aggregate usage patterns (e.g., number of visitors, top pages, device types). Both tools are privacy-first: they do not use cookies, do not fingerprint users, and do not share data with advertising networks. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

4. Cookies

The Service does not set any cookies. The localStorage item described above is not a cookie and is not transmitted to any server.

5. Third-Party Services

The Service loads fonts from Google Fonts. Google may collect limited data (including IP address) as part of serving font files. See Google's Privacy Policy for details. No other third-party scripts, trackers, or SDKs are loaded by the Service.

6. Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to retain or delete. The gc_seen localStorage flag persists in your browser until you clear your browser data. You can remove it at any time through your browser's developer tools or storage settings.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the Service, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data. Since we collect no personal data, most requests will be trivially satisfied. However, if you have questions about data held by our hosting provider or analytics services, please contact us and we will assist to the extent possible.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact
