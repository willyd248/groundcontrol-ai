Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026 · Effective immediately

This Privacy Policy describes how GroundControl AI ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and handles information when you use our Service at groundcontrol-ai.vercel.app. We are committed to being transparent about our minimal data practices.

1. Information We Collect

GroundControl AI is a client-side simulation with no user accounts, no login, and no form submissions. We collect minimal data:

  • Usage data via Vercel Analytics: Vercel, our hosting provider, automatically collects anonymized page view data including referrer, country-level location, browser type, and device type. This data does not include personally identifiable information and is used solely for understanding site traffic.
  • Local Storage: We store a single key (gc_seen) in your browser's localStorage to remember whether you have dismissed the onboarding overlay. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Name, email address, or any personally identifiable information
  • Payment or financial information
  • Account credentials (there are no accounts)
  • Precise geolocation data
  • Behavioral tracking or cross-site tracking data

3. Cookies

GroundControl AI does not set any cookies directly. Vercel Analytics uses a privacy-preserving approach that does not rely on cookies for visitor identification. Google Fonts, used for typography, may set cookies according to Google's own privacy policy. You can disable Google Fonts requests by blocking fonts.googleapis.com in your browser or network settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Vercel — hosting and anonymous analytics. See Vercel Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — web typography (JetBrains Mono). See Google Privacy Policy.

We do not use advertising networks, social media trackers, or any analytics service that creates persistent user profiles.

5. Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our servers. Vercel Analytics retains anonymized aggregate traffic data according to Vercel's own retention policies. localStorage data (gc_seen) persists in your browser until you clear your browser data.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personally identifiable information, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete on our end. To clear the localStorage preference set by the site, you can clear your browser's site data for groundcontrol-ai.vercel.app in your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided information to the Service, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact Will DiMaio via GitHub.

GroundControl AI is a simulation product. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to JFK International Airport, Port Authority of New York and New Jersey, or any airline or aviation authority.
