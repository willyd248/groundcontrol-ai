Questions

Frequently asked.

What are AI airport ground operations?

AI airport ground operations use machine learning to automate and optimize ramp activities including vehicle dispatch, gate assignments, fueling routes, and baggage handling. GroundControl AI simulates this in real time, showing how AI reduces aircraft turnaround times and prevents runway incursions.

How does airport dispatch optimization work?

Airport dispatch optimization uses algorithms to assign ground support vehicles — tugs, fuel trucks, catering units — to arriving and departing aircraft based on proximity, task priority, gate load, and runway safety state. GroundControl AI demonstrates this with a live simulation that processes inbound flight queues and issues optimized dispatch orders in real time.

What is a ground handling simulation?

A ground handling simulation models the full lifecycle of aircraft servicing between landing and takeoff — including vehicle routing, crew coordination, fueling, catering, and pushback. GroundControl AI provides a browser-based radar simulation with 18+ vehicle types, live dispatch logs, and a Runway Safety System panel.

Can AI reduce airport turnaround times?

Yes. By optimizing ground vehicle dispatch and minimizing idle time, AI systems can reduce aircraft gate turnaround times by 10–25%. GroundControl AI visualizes this optimization in a real-time simulation.

Is this free to play?

Completely free. No account, no download, no ads. GroundControl AI runs entirely in your browser as a single HTML file with zero external dependencies.
